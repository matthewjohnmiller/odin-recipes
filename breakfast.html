Breakfast

Description

I just love this recipe. It's got pasta, it's got meatballs, it's got the whole shebang!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Meatballs
  • Parmesean Cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. Meanwhile, pour frozen meatballs into a skillet on medium heat.
  3. Heat frozen meatballs for 8-10 minutes.
  4. When the water has reached a boil, pour box of pasta into boiling water.
  5. Cook pasta 9-11 minutes for "al dente" and 10-12 minutes for softer pasta.
  6. Plate and serve with Parmesean cheese.
